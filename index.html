I've been dedicated to design journey at least 4 years, begin from the time I just get into the university. Since that, I've loved to jump outside my comfort zone. As people say, “ If you don’t give it a try, you’ll never know”. In 2019 summer vacation, I had a working holiday in 小琉球, an island which is part of Taiwan, for 4 weeks. I met a lot of new friends who also was in a working holiday. The more I see, the more I'll know. Because of the journey, I find out that I love to making people happy. I'm a happy guy in a group. I want know more different kinds of people. Now, I'm still keeping create new life experience, and trying apply it to design, and strives to be a empathetic guy who likes to try something new.
