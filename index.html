ABOUT ME

YOU’LL NEVER KNOW
JUST GIVE IT A TRY

Hi, I’m 王俊鎔, Aaron, a UX designer. Or, you can just call me Aaron, read as my Taiwanese nickname. I have been devoted to make people enjoy product at least 4 years, begin from the time I just get into the university. Since that, I have loved to jump outside my comfort zone. As people say, “ If you don’t give it a try, you’ll never know” The more I see, the more I’ll know. In 2019 summer vacation, I’ve been to 小琉球, an island which is part of Taiwan, alone for 4 weeks. It’s a kind of working holiday, which is work for dorm. During the 4 weeks in 小琉球, I met a lot of people and made about 10 more friends who was like me. They always said:”You’re really a such open-minded and friendly guy”. And I still remember the day my contract was expired, my boss gaves me bonus and said she admired my passion in work. After this experience, I realized a pharse, “Attitude determines your height”. Now, I strives to be a humble guy who likes to try something new.
